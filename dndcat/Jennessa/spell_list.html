  • Summoning

      Confession

      • Level 5
      • Summoning spell
      • Duration - 1 hour
      • Cast Time - 1 action
      • V,S, M - a drop of caster's blood
      • Range cast - 60 feet
      • Target - creatures in front of you that you can see.

      Targets and caster step out of time and converse. Targets need to make WIS save or be forced to participate. At this time the caster can ask the targets of what would they want in return of occasional service to the caster. The spell ends with duration, refusal, or acceptance of a contract. If the spell caster is knocked out the spell ends and the caster has 1 hp and is in the same place and time where the spell began.

      Summon

      • Level (CR/2, rounded down)
      • Summoning spell
      • Duration - 1 hour
      • Cast time - 1 action
      • V,S,M - a drop of caster's bloodl
      • Range - 60 feet

      Caster calls forth a creature that is contracted in the spell book. The creature tries to follow the request of the caster to the best of it's ability. The creature has free will as long as it's CR is greater than the caster's level. If the CR is lower than caster's level then the creature regards the requests as commands. If the creature drops below zero HP during the summon it will go back to where it came from with one HP and no longer is bound by the contract. The creature can always choose to keep the contract even if it falls in battle during the summon. The spell ends when the duration is up or if creature's or caster's HP drops to zero.
