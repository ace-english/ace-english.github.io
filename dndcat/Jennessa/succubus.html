Succubus Level 3:

  • Jennessa has advantage on all social checks against men.
  • She also gains a +1 to insight
  • She's much curvier than she used to be...

Buffs

  • Sated. From completing a Bloodlust. +2 AC, +4d8 HP, Regenerate 20, +2 to attack, +2 on spell save DCs, Wings (90 ft fly speed)
  • Lesser Sated. From draining a life. +1 AC, +2d8 HP, Regenerate 10, +1 to attack, +1 on spell saves, claw attack (see Bloodlust)

Bloodlust

  • If Jennessa doesn't feed once a week, she may lose control. If it has been a year, she loses control for a full twenty-four hours.
  • Stats:
    • STR: 27
    • DEX: 23
    • CON: 20
    • INT: same
    • WIS: 10
    • CHA: 25
  • Jennessa has advantage on all saving throws.
  • Jennessa gains a temporary maximum HP equal to the sum of all the life she has drained during her rampage.
  • Mulitattack. 2 claws and a bite
    • Bite: +12 6d6+12 slashing. Lifedrain
    • Claw: +12 5d8+12 piercing plus 1d4 Constitution damage. Lifedrain.
  • Frightful/Seductive Presence. Anyone in a 60 foot radius must succeed on a Wisdom save (8+Charisma+proficiency= DC 20) or be frightened or seduced (Jennessa's choice)
  • Speed. 50 ft move speed. After 20 kills Jennessa gets wings which grant her 90 ft fly speed.
  • Resistance.Cold, Lightning, Acid, as well as bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from non-magic weapons.
  • Immunity. Fire
  • *Completion of the bloodlust is subjective

Feeding

  • Kiss - 2d8 life drain. - 10exp
  • Make out - 4d8 life drain. - 50exp
  • Touch - 1d4 life drain. - 1exp
  • Hand job - 1 lvl drain. - 200exp
  • Blow job - 1 lvl drain + 2d8 life drain. - 300exp
  • Penetrating sex - 2 lvl drain. - 500exp
  • Kill by sex - 1000exp + bonuses.
  • First time bedding someone - extra 100exp
